Mouse Operation

Pepakura Designer is mainly operated by your mouse. A mouse with a wheel enables easier operation of the software. If your mouse doesn't have a wheel, a combination of keyboard and mouse allows you the same operation as a mouse with a wheel.

The functions on the screens of the 3D model window and 2D pattern window are as follows. Learn them in order to work smoothly and efficiently. Since this description of the operations is shown on the status bar, it will be helpful to you.

3D Model Window

Edit operation such as edge selection Left Click
Shows pop-up menu Right Click
3D model rotate Right Drag
Pan Center Button (Wheel) Drag or [Ctrl] + Right Drag
Zoom In/Out Wheel or [Shift] + Right Drag

2D Pattern Window

Edit operation such as edge or face selection Left Click
Edit operation such as part or text movement Left Drag
Shows pop-up menu Right Click
Pan Right Drag or Center Button (Wheel) Drag or [Ctrl] + Right Drag
Zoom In/Out Wheel or [Shift] + Right Drag
