Problem of Screen Display

There might be something wrong with the display, such as the window turning black.

There might also be problems such as a display failure, or the application might freeze because of incompatibility with your version of Opengl32.dll and your video card. In that case, make sure that a new driver is available from the video card's vendor. Problems may be resolved by updating your video card driver.

If a problem still persists, try the following measures:
1. Right-click on a blank part of your desktop, and select [Property].
2. Select [Details] of [Setting] tab.
3. Reduce the level of [Hardware Acceleration] on the [Troubleshooting] tab.

(The above steps may differ according to your version of Windows.)

Similar adjustments may be made by [Start]-[Control Panel]-[System]-[Performance]-[Graphics].
