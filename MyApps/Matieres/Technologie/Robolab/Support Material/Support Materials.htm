Summary of Steps: LEGO MINDSTORMS FOR SCHOOLS Hardware Setup

After installing the ROBOLAB software, choose Setup from the Main Menu.

After reviewing Setup, you should have:

1) Connected a Serial or USB Transmitter to the COM or USB port on the back of the computer.

2) Installed 6 AA batteries in the RCX.

3) Reviewed how to download the firmware to the RCX using ROBOLAB.

Now continue the hardware setup and follow the steps below to download firmware to the RCX. Firmware enables the communication between the computer and the RCX.

1) Double-click the ROBOLAB icon to start the software.

2) When the Main Menu appears, click Run ROBOLAB.

3) When the menu screen appears, click Administrator.

4) When the Administrator screen appears, click Download Firmware. Follow the on-screen instructions.

5) When the download is successful, the RCX beeps and ’00.00’ shows on the RCX display.

6) On the computer screen, a dialog box appears confirming that the communication was successful. Click OK to close the dialog box.

7) When the Administrator screen appears, click the Back button.

8) Click Quit to close ROBOLAB.

9) Click Quit again to confirm and close ROBOLAB.

10) Double-click the ROBOLAB icon to start the software again.

11) You will be taken back to the last step of Setup. Here you can confirm whether the system is working or not.

12) Click Main Menu to leave Setup.

13) Click Training Missions to learn how to use ROBOLAB.
