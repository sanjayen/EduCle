The teacher guide contains useful information for the teacher that will a resource mutualized by other teachers.

It is available on the left drawer indicated with a tab containing an icon showing a teacher hat.

This teacher guide offers you the possibility to :

  • mention the different authors of the resources as well as the copyrights of the used resources,

  • give the information of the session (title, pedagogic objectives, the student or teacher activities...),

  • describe the resource (school level, curriculum, keywords, license of the resource...),

  • propose the actions to be realized by the teachers or the students,

  • store additional or optional elements (image, sound, videos, application, web link, …) as well as the corrected exercises if necessary.

The information contained in the teacher guide therefore belong to the document and allows the users to get a better overview of the pedagogic frame in which the activity is being proposed.

NB : if you export your Open-Sankoré document in the PDF format, the teacher guide will not be, for the moment, included.
