A new document is created each time you launch Open-Sankoré. It appears in the folder «Untitled Documents» in the «Document» mode (left column).

To facilitate the work of the users, Open-Sankoré automatically saves your work everytime a page is changed or when the software is quit. Therefore, there is not “Save” function.

The folder “Models” lets you drop presentations that you wish to conserve without them to be modifiable. You will have to drage them to another folder to duplicate them and edit them.

In the “Document” mode, it is possible to :

  • Rename the document

  • Create folders and sub-folders

  • Import or export Open-Sankoré documents (*.ubz) or the folders containing documents (*.ubx)

  • Add .pdf to your pages

  • Add folder of images

  • Reuse pages from a presentation

  • ...
